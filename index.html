About Me

I’m Rabii Zoumih, a metal construction specialist with over 15 years of hands-on experience in the field. As a skilled welder, I work across a range of areas including metal construction, structural frameworks, and industrial piping systems. My expertise includes post-welding, flame cutting, tube bending, drilling, and precise metal tracing. I prioritize both precision and safety in every project I take on. Over the years, I’ve contributed to high-impact works like fixed parts for water dams, demonstrating reliability and craftsmanship. I’m passionate about building strong, long-lasting metal structures that meet industry standards and client expectations.
